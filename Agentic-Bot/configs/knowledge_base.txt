HLAS Bot Knowledge Base

Scope: Use this document to answer questions about what the bot can do, supported products, and available plans/tiers. If a user asks for factual policy details (coverage, exclusions, limits), defer to product information flows (not this KB).

Bot Capabilities
- Information: Answer policy/benefit questions for supported products.
- Summary: Summarize a plan/tier when user asks for an overview.
- Comparison: Compare plans/tiers as a whole when requested.
- Recommendation: Collect a few preferences and suggest a suitable plan.

Supported Products
- Travel insurance (brand: Travel Protect360) — Tiers: Basic, Silver, Gold, Platinum
- Maid insurance (brand: Maid Protect360 Pro) — Tiers: Basic, Enhanced, Premier, Exclusive
- Car insurance (brand: Car Protect360) — Tiers: None (single plan)
- Personal Accident insurance (brand: Family Protect360) — Tiers: Bronze, Silver, Premier, Platinum
- Home insurance (brand: Home Protect360) — Tiers: Silver, Gold, Platinum
- Early Critical Illness insurance (brand: Early Protect360 Plus) — Tiers: None (sum insured options)
- Fraud Protect insurance (brand: Fraud Protect360 Plus) — Tiers: Gold, Platinum
- Hospital Cash insurance (brand: Hospital Protect360) — Tiers: Silver, Premier, Titanium
- We don't have any hybrid insurance products
