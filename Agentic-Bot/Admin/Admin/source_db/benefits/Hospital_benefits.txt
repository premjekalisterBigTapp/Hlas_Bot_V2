For a Single Hospital insurance plan, a Monthly payment type receives a 0% discount.
For a Single Hospital insurance plan, an Annual payment type receives a 5% discount.
For a Couple Hospital insurance plan, a Monthly payment type receives a 5% discount.
For a Couple Hospital insurance plan, an Annual payment type receives a 10% discount.
Under the Hospital insurance, the Daily Hospital Cash benefit is provided per day for up to 365 days, with a maximum payable amount of S$100 for the Silver plan, S$200 for the Premier plan, and S$300 for the Titanium plan.
Under the Hospital insurance, the Triple Hospital Cash benefit for stays in the Intensive Care Unit (ICU) is provided per day for up to 30 days, with a maximum payable amount of S$300 for the Silver plan, S$600 for the Premier plan, and S$900 for the Titanium plan.
Under the Hospital insurance, the Recuperation Benefit is provided per day for up to 30 days, with a maximum payable amount of S$50 for the Silver plan, S$75 for the Premier plan, and S$100 for the Titanium plan.
Under the Hospital insurance, the Special COVID-19 Benefit has a maximum benefit payable of S$10,000 for the Silver, Premier, and Titanium plans.
Under the Hospital insurance, the Ambulance and Transportation expenses benefit has a maximum benefit payable of S$500 for the Silver, Premier, and Titanium plans.
Under the Hospital insurance, the No Claim Bonus is 20% for the Silver, Premier, and Titanium plans.
